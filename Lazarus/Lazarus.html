Tokenomics

Buy-Back

The Lazarus team will buy back LZR tokens from the market and burn them, reducing the total supply of LZR tokens and increasing the value of each token.

Liquidity

The Lazarus team will add LZR tokens to liquidity pools on Uniswap and PancakeSwap, increasing the demand for LZR tokens and increasing the value of each token.

Treasury

The Lazarus team will use a portion of the LZR tokens generated from the buy-back and liquidity pools to fund the development of the Lazarus platform.

Gaming

The Lazarus team will use a portion of the LZR tokens generated from the buy-back and liquidity pools to fund the development of the Lazarus platform.
